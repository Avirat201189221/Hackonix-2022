About Us

ISTE is the abode to a passionate community that believes in harnessing the power of technology to transform teaching and learning,accelerate innovation and solve tough problems in education.

Over the course of one exciting weekend, people from a range of disciplines will come together in teams to try and solve real world problems.

Who We Are

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading national professional non-profit organization devoted to the overall development of our Technical Education System. With its government recognition and a legacy of 50 years, we feel that we can provide a highly constructive environment for someone who is driven by the will to leave a mark.

What We Do

We, at ISTE, assist and contribute to the production and development of top-quality professional engineers and technicians required by industry and other organizations by providing guidance and training to students through various workshops and events that polish and test their skills.

What We Believe In

ISTE is an abode to a passionate community that believes in harnessing the power of technology to transform teaching and learning, accelerate innovation and solve tough problems in education. The team inspires the creation of solutions and connections that improve opportunities for all learners by delivering practical guidance, evidence-based professional learning, and thought-provoking events.

Rulebook

Pre-Event: Stock Overflow

  • Rule 1 :

    To compete each user will have to register on the ISTE tournament on the StockPe app

  • Rule 2 :

    All the people on the tournament will be given 1,00,000 virtual coins to start with.

  • Rule 3 :

    They will have access to trade (buy and sell stocks) in Nifty 75 stocks with those virtual coins

  • Rule 4 :

    There will be a love leaderboard that will show all the users their rankings based on the profits they make.

  • Rule 5 :

    At the end of the tournament the final profits will be calculated and final winner will be visible on the app's leaderboard

FAQs

What is a Hackathon?

What is a Hackathon?

An Event where you solve problem statments in a time period (here 24hrs)

Any prerequisites ? Any prerequisites ?

There are no specific prerequisites to joining a hackathon, just put forward the best ideas you have

Do I need a team ? Do I need a team ?

Yes, you will need at least 1 teammate and can have a maximum of 5 team members

Are there prizes? Are there prizes

Yes, there will be many prizes in different fields such as: The Track Winners, The best First Year Hack or The Best Monetizable Idea

How much does it cost to attend ? How much does it cost to attend ?

There is no cost for registration or additional cost for participation in the event

What should I bring ? What should I bring ?

Try to bring your team, a laptop and a positive attitude :)
